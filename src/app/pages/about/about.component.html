About Us:

CCW 360 initiative is, collaboration of IT with CPS teams to reduce turn around time of the incidents. After analyzing whole bunch of inflow incidents, we have categorized and automated the scenarios which lead to number of incidents, so that they can be validated directly into this system. It includes multiple scenarios across the tracks like Deals, Configurations, Estimate and Orders.

We have arranged all scenarios in application--Module--Scenario Structure for intuitive navigation. For Ex: If you are facing issue with Reusable Non Standard Deal discounts, you can look into Deals--Reusable Non Standard Deal issues module. While we have tried to arrange as logically as possible, if you feel it can be better categorized please do let us know.

Happy using CPS Inquiry System. Please give us feedback, suggestions so we can improve it to serve you better!
